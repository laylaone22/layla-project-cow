Hindquarter

Hindquarter CUTS

Beef from the hind leg is leaner then loin and forequarter cuts. It yields many fine steaks but slightly cheaper steaks and roasts as well as braising cuts.

BONELESS RUMPBridging the sirloin and leg, the rump is well marbled and makes excellent steaks and roasts. The thin muscle lying over the top is sold as minute steak or picanha.

TOP ROUND STEAKNot as tender or well-marbled as loin cuts, nevertheless top round makes good roasts and steaks. The thin muscle lying overtop is sometimes sold separatly as top round cap or top cap.

SLICED SHANKThis lean, boned out shin makes a hearty stew when diced, and has a lovely silky texture.
